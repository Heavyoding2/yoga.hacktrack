Yoga is a mind and body practice. Various styles of yoga combine physical postures, breathing techniques, and meditation or relaxation. Yoga is an ancient practice that may have originated in India. It involves movement, meditation, and breathing techniques to promote mental and physical well-being.

SUKHASANA

Sukhasana, also referred to as easy pose, is a yoga asana performed in a seated position. Easy pose aids in opening the hips and aligning the spine, and its simplicity makes it suitable for yogis of all skill levels, including beginners. Potential Benefits of the Sukhasana Pose Pain relief: The pose can ease lower back and knee pain. Improving mobility: You can unlock the knees, hips, and ankles. Relaxation: Sukhasana can help you feel calm and promote tranquility

VAJRASANA

Vajrasana pose is a simple sitting yoga pose. Its name comes from the Sanskrit word vajra, which means thunderbolt or diamond. ... Some benefits of Vajrasana also include: 1.aiding in digestion. 2.Relieving or preventing constipation. 3.Strengthening pelvic muscles.
